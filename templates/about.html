About & Help

Our Mission

Volunteer Portal exists to empower individuals and strengthen communities by making it simple to discover, join, and manage local volunteer opportunities. We believe everyone has something valuable to give—time, skills, or compassion—and our platform connects that goodwill with organizations in need. Join us in fostering a culture of service, collaboration, and positive impact.

How It Works

  1. Log In with Google for secure, one-click access.
  2. Browse Events by category or date to find what fits you.
  3. Sign Up instantly—your spot is reserved!
  4. Manage your profile, preferences, and cancel if plans change.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my contact preferences?

Go to your Dashboard → Edit Profile. Check or uncheck Email/SMS and save.

Can I cancel my signup?

Yes—on your Dashboard under “Your Sign-Ups,” click Cancel next to the event.

Who can create events?

Admins (password-protected) can add, edit, or delete events via the Admin panel.

I’m having trouble logging in. Help!

Make sure pop-ups are allowed and your Google account is active. Contact us at support@volunteer-portal.com.
